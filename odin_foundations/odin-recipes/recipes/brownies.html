Make the best brownies:

This is a good brownies recipe.

Ingredients

  • Flour -- 70g
  • Sugar -- 90g/110g(Dark chocolate/milk chocolate)
  • Eggs -- 2 no.
  • Butter - 100g
  • Chocolate - 120g
  • salt -- 0.7g(a dash is recommended)
  • vanilla -- as per requirement (1/2g)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the Oven at 180oC
  2. Start with preparing the chocolate. Since it needs to be melted, if stating with a slab, chop the chocolate or break into smaller pieces. Melt over a water bath with butter.
  3. Now add the eggs in the mixer and attach paddle. Keep at low speed and start adding suger. Add little at a time, don't break the flow of the batter.
  4. Once the sugar is added start adding the melting chocolate and butter slowly.
  5. Now sift the flour (add salt/flavorings or starch at this stage if using) and start folding the batter with a spatula. A small amount should be added at a time like previous steps. Mixing speed is slow throughout the process.
  6. Pour in the pan and keep in oven for 30-40 minutes at 180oC
  7. Chill at room temperature until ready to serve. Best served with coffee or Ice cream.
