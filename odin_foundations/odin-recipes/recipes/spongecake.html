Sponge Cake Recipe

The truth of the matter is that, if you can make a sponge cake, you can make any cake. A sponge cake is the simplest of all and you never have to remember the recipe. The ratio of the ingredirents is 100:100:100 i.e. there are only 3 ingredients. Flour, sugar, eggs in equal amounts.

ingredients

  • Flour -- 100g
  • Sugar -- 100g
  • Eggs -- 100g(seperate egg yolks for better results)
  • salt/vanilla/flavorings -- as req

Steps:

  1. Start with the egg whites. Add them to the mixture and attach a whisk. Whisk for about 1.5-3 mins until it starts foaming up.
  2. Start adding the sugar slowly, and let the foaming happen on high speed. This should take 4-8 mins. Stop at stiff peak, so keep checking the cream peak.
  3. Once achieved a stiff peak. Take the flour, sift it and start folding it in your cream. Slowly. dont't break the flow of the batter.
  4. Add flavorings as required and a dash of salt is recommended.
  5. Optionally, fold in the egg yolks for a nice color on crust.
